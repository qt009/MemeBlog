What are Memes?

A meme is an idea, behavior, or style that spreads from person to person within a culture, often in the form of an image or video on the internet. If you want to learn more, click here to learn more

Types of Memes

Memes can be classified into different types based on their content and "dankness". The three main types of memes are:

  • Funny memes - These memes are meant to be funny and maybe cringy as hell
  • Cute memes - These memes are usually images of adorable animals (like a cat) that boast "cuteness".
  • Dark memes - These memes are maybe more or less sensitive but still quite enjoyable
